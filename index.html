Fizco - Return and Refund Policy

At Fizco, we strive to ensure your complete satisfaction with our products. Please review our return and refund policies below.

Returns

The eligibility for returns is based on the product type and condition. Please refer to the individual product pages for detailed return policies.

  • Condition for Returns: Products must be returned in their original condition, unused, and with all tags and packaging intact.
  • Return Period: Returns must be initiated within 5 days from the date of delivery.

Refunds

Refund policies may vary depending on the product. Please check the individual product details for specific refund terms.

  • Refund Process: Once your return is received and inspected, we will notify you of the approval or rejection of your refund. If approved, the refund will be processed, and a credit will automatically be applied to your original method of payment within 2 business days.
  • Refund Amount: The refund amount will depend on the condition of the returned product and any applicable restocking fees.

Shipping

All products are available for local pickup or delivery. Delivery will be made within 5 working days from the date of order.

For any questions or further assistance, please contact our customer support team.
